Campaign Basics

A Moment is a short, high-attention marketing burst. Recommended flight is 3 to 5 days to maximize saturation.
Moment Type
Product Launch
New vehicle / model debut
Tentpole / Holiday
Key seasonal sales
Recommended length: 3 to 5 days.
$ USD
Automotive Industry Benchmark: For a new vehicle launch campaign covering the US market with a 3–5 day flight, the recommended budget range is $500,000 – $1,500,000. Premium / luxury segment launches typically require $800,000+ for optimal saturation (40%–60% reach). Budget below $300,000 may result in insufficient frequency for awareness goals.
